Eastern Lights Industries presents our comprehensive development plan for building India's next-generation data center infrastructure. This strategic roadmap outlines our vision for creating sustainable, high-performance computing facilities that will power India's digital transformation while supporting our commitment to environmental responsibility.

Strategic Vision

Our development plan is built on three core pillars: sustainability, innovation, and community impact. We aim to establish Eastern Lights Industries as India's leading provider of green data center solutions, leveraging our unique position in renewable energy-rich regions to deliver unparalleled performance at competitive costs.

Phase 1: Foundation (2025-2026)

The initial phase focuses on establishing our core infrastructure and partnerships. This includes:

  • Site Selection: Identifying optimal locations near renewable energy sources
  • Technology Partnerships: Establishing relationships with leading hardware manufacturers
  • Team Building: Assembling world-class engineering and operations teams
  • Pilot Facilities: Launching initial 10MW data center in Gujarat

Phase 2: Expansion (2026-2028)

Building on our foundation, we will scale our operations across multiple states:

  • Multi-State Presence: Establishing facilities in Rajasthan, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu
  • Technology Innovation: Implementing advanced liquid cooling and AI-optimized architectures
  • Capacity Growth: Scaling to 100MW+ across all locations
  • Service Expansion: Launching specialized AI training and HPC services

Phase 3: Leadership (2028-2030)

Achieving market leadership through innovation and scale:

  • Next-Generation Facilities: 500MW+ total capacity with cutting-edge technologies
  • Research & Development: Leading innovation in sustainable computing
  • Global Partnerships: Collaborating with international technology leaders
  • Industry Standards: Setting benchmarks for green data center operations

Environmental Impact

Our development plan prioritizes environmental sustainability:

100% Renewable Energy

All facilities will be powered exclusively by renewable sources, with direct connections to solar, wind, and hydroelectric generation.

Carbon Neutral Operations

Advanced cooling technologies and energy-efficient designs will minimize our carbon footprint while maximizing computational performance.

Economic Development

Beyond technology, our plan includes significant community and economic benefits:

  • Job Creation: 5,000+ high-tech positions across India
  • Skills Development: Training programs for next-generation engineers
  • Local Partnerships: Collaboration with regional businesses and institutions
  • Economic Growth: Contributing billions to India's GDP through technology and innovation

Timeline & Milestones

Our aggressive yet achievable timeline ensures rapid progress:

Q4 2025

Site acquisition and initial construction in Gujarat

Q2 2026

First 10MW facility operational

2027

Expansion to Rajasthan and Karnataka

2028

100MW total capacity achieved

2030

Industry leadership with 500MW+ capacity
