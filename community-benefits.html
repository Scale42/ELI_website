We believe business should be environmentally and socially sustainable - so at Eastern Lights Industries we engage with the communities in which we locate our data centres. We strive to build up an Indian workforce with technical skills and experience relevant for the AI revolution. Our projects bring opportunities to train and gain practical experience in cutting-edge technologies that are shaping India's digital future.

Building Technical Workforce for the AI Revolution

India's AI revolution demands a skilled workforce capable of operating and maintaining advanced computing infrastructure. At Eastern Lights Industries, we actively contribute to this workforce development by providing comprehensive training programs in:

  • Data Centre Operations & Management: Hands-on experience with modern data centre technologies, cooling systems, and power management
  • Accelerated Computing Architectures: Training in GPU, TPU, and ASIC technologies that power modern AI applications
  • Advanced Machine Learning (ML) and AI: Practical experience with AI workloads, model training, and inference optimization

Bringing Opportunities to Remote Communities

Our unique ability to deploy data centres into remote regions brings the opportunities of the AI-driven global technology boom to some of India's most remote and rural communities. This addresses the critical challenge of talent distribution, where technical skills are concentrated in urban centres while many communities lack access to technology careers.

Workforce Development Impact

By establishing data centre operations in remote locations, we create sustainable career opportunities and help bridge the urban-rural technology divide. Our training programs equip local talent with globally relevant skills, enabling participation in India's AI revolution regardless of geographic location.

Renewable Energy Development

Our data centre projects also stimulate development of renewable energy projects as off-take customers for otherwise stranded electrical power awaiting grid connection. This creates a virtuous cycle where:

  • Economic Viability: Data centres provide guaranteed demand for renewable energy projects
  • Grid Independence: Remote communities gain access to reliable, clean power
  • Energy Security: Local renewable projects reduce dependence on distant power sources
  • Environmental Benefits: Increased renewable energy utilization reduces carbon emissions

Community Engagement and Development
